Obvious consequences

  • Technical compatibility ( -moz -webkit -o )
  • UX patterns differ across operating systems, browsers, etc.

  • Some things suck

  • Responsive design

Not-so-obvious consequences

Not-so-obvious consequences

Censorship, choosing when not to censor is a huge political tool, drives new economies of copy-cat software, social networks.

Designing for the flexible web is not just about compatibility, but also being aware of the political and moral choices of your design decisions relative to different groups of users.
